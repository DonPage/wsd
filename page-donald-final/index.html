About Us

The Red Secrets Bar is a bar founded by the Apes in the earliest of days, some say it was the first place to go when trying to get 'drunk' We were able to keep this place running exceptional well once we evolved into Humans, some might even say the Red Secrets Bar may be the reason we evolved so quickly. To keep up with tradition, we enforce new ways of getting drunk and eating so we Humans will evolve once more.

But unlike in the early days where The Red Secrets was open to everyone, we now enforce a invite only membership and all employees have to pay $430 application fee for background check. This was put inplace to keep balance in order and traditions pure.

How To Join

To join this exclusive bar you must have a already active member vouch for you. Once you get the voutches you need you will be ranked as a trail member. During this period you will go through a series of challenges to test your worthiness.

If you need to know how much the food cost then don't join.
