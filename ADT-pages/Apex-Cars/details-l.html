Lamborghini Gallardo

They started with one of the best and most innovative cars in the company's history - the Gallardo - and changed (read: improved) almost everything about the car. Power bumped from 525 to 560hp; Suspension tweaked to be both more comfortable and better-performing; Exhaust tuned to aural perfection; Body panels subtly (but aggressively) modified; Headlights (with integrated LEDs) redesigned; Rear fascia facelifted.
